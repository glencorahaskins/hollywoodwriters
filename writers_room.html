The potential to undermine the career ladder and opportunities for newer writers

If the use of generative AI results in smaller, or even nonexistent, writers’ room, it would threaten the structured career ladder that has defined TV writing in Hollywood for decades. Historically, if writers are successful in securing roles in successive seasons or on new shows, they typically ascend the following hierarchy, gaining valuable experience, mentorship, credits and on-the-job training in each role.


In addition to the roles above, which are represented by the Writers’ Guild, the show’s writing is also supported by entry-level roles like writers’ assistant and personal assistant, which often provide a springboard to staff writing positions with union representation.
